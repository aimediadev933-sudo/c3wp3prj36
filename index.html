Key Activities

1Regional Survey

129 responses collected across 15 countries to assess female participation in network engineering and identify key barriers and opportunities.

2Technical Webinars

5 specialized topics delivered to 2,389 participants with focused engagement of 604 female professionals and students.

3Hands-on Workshops

3 in-person workshops conducted in Bangladesh, providing intensive technical training to 122 women in network engineering.

4Fellowship Program

9 fellowships awarded to outstanding TechGirls and Future IT-Girls for advanced training and leadership development.

5Digital Community

Created comprehensive online support network through dedicated website, WhatsApp groups, and Facebook communities for ongoing collaboration.

6Outreach Materials

700 promotional brochures distributed across the region to raise awareness and encourage female participation in network engineering.
